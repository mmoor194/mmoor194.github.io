Peanut And Jelly Sandwich

Description

Things that are need to make the Peanut and Jelly Sandwich

Jelly, Peanut's and bread and a spoon to spread the Peanut and jelly on the Sandwich

Ingredients

  • bread
  • Peanut Butter
  • Jelly

Instructions

  1. Get bread on cutting board (x2 slices)
  2. cut bread in whatever shape you want
  3. get spoon and spread Peanut Butter on bread
  4. once spreaded Peanut Butter on bread smear Jelly on the bread
  5. once done all of this put the second slice of bread onto the over peace then you have a peanut butter Sandwich
